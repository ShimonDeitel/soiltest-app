Privacy Policy

Soiltest is an offline-first app. All entries you create — including any text, dates, and photos you attach — are stored only in the app's local storage on your own device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data. There are no analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs in this app.

The only network connection this app makes is to Apple's App Store servers to process optional Pro subscription purchases via StoreKit. Apple's own privacy policy governs that transaction; we never see your payment details.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is deleted with it.
